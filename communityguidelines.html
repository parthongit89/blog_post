Stillness Community Guidelines

Last Updated: May 2026 Legal & Ethics

Welcome to the Stillness community! Our mission is to create a thoughtful, respectful, and inspiring space for writers and readers to share ideas, knowledge, and perspectives. To maintain a high-quality environment where everyone feels safe to express themselves, all users are required to adhere to the following guidelines. By creating an account or publishing content on Stillness, you agree to these rules.

01

Respect and Civility

We believe in the power of discourse. However, this must always be grounded in mutual respect. We have Zero Tolerance for Hate Speech, harassment, or bullying of any kind.

Engage in Constructive Discussion. Critique the idea, not the person. Any comments or essays found to be intentionally inflammatory or targeting specific individuals/groups will be removed immediately.

02

Content Authenticity and Integrity

No Plagiarism: Your work must be your own. Properly attribute all quotes, sources, and inspiration. Lifting content without permission or attribution is a violation of our core values.

Avoid Misinformation: While we encourage diverse perspectives, we do not allow the spread of demonstrably false health, scientific, or civic information that could cause real-world harm.

Responsible AI Use: If your content is significantly generated by AI, it must be clearly disclosed to the reader. Authenticity is what our community values most.

03

Safety and Legal Compliance

We comply with all local and international laws. Do not use Stillness for Illegal Activities or to promote self-harm, violence, or illegal substances.

Protect Personal Privacy (Doxing): Do not share private information of others (addresses, phone numbers, private emails) without their explicit consent.

Keep it Safe for Work (NSFW): Stillness is an editorial space intended for a broad professional and academic audience. Excessive graphic violence or sexually explicit content is prohibited.

04

Spam and Commercial Promotion

Stillness is a place for ideas, not a marketplace for junk. No Link Spamming or repetitive commercial posts. While you may link to your personal projects or services if relevant to the content, the primary focus must be on the quality of the essay.

We value Authentic Engagement. Use of bots, engagement pods, or "follow-for-follow" schemes to manipulate metrics is strictly forbidden.

05

Enforcement and Consequences

We take these guidelines seriously. Violations may result in one or more of the following actions at our sole discretion:

  • warning Issue formal warning to the account holder.
  • visibility_off Temporary or permanent unpublishing of violating content.
  • block Immediate suspension or termination of user accounts.
