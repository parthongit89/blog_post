Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to Stillness. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data When you register, we collect the personal details you provide, such as your username, email address, and password (which is securely encrypted).
User-Generated Content We store the articles, blogs, text, and images you explicitly upload and publish on the platform.
Cookies & Local Storage We use local storage and basic cookies to keep you securely logged into your session so you don't have to re-enter your password on every page.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • check_circle To maintain and manage your account and user profile.
  • check_circle To display your author name alongside the articles you publish.
  • check_circle To ensure the security and proper functioning of our platform.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, as Stillness is currently a trial/experimental blogging application, users are advised not to use sensitive or high-security passwords shared with other critical real-world accounts.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with outside parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to view, update, or delete your account information and your published articles at any time through your profile settings.
